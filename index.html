Kawaii Cakes

Custom Cakes for All

Welcome! We are a family-owned company based out of El Paso Texas. Kawaii Cakes can help you make any event a bit more special by having a custom cake created for you. We can customize the flavor, size, and theme of your cake to meet your event's needs.

How does it work? We will start with a consultation with our shop's owner. With her help you can create a cake that will not only look good but taste great too! Kawaii Cakes has a large range of flavors from the traditional (vanilla and chocolate) to more unexpected flavors (Nutella and Cookies and Cream). All orders are made based on the number of people being served and the location of the event.If you have a design in mind you can submit your photos so we can provide guidance on your order and bring it to live. We hope to hear from you soon and create a Kawaii Cake for you!
